Customize the look and feel of the source editor

Balthisar Tidy for Work gives you many, many options to customize the look and feel of your working environment. For example these features are all available to suit your working habits and aesthetic tastes:

  • View Source HTML and Tidy HTML panels in an over-under fashion or in a side-by-side manner.

  • Turn syntax highlighting on or off.

  • Show or hide invisible characters in the editor.

  • Show or hide the line number gutter.

  • View error locations directly within the Source HTML text.

  • Turn the Tidy Options and/or Tidy Messages panel on or off.

  • Change the appearance, sort order, grouping, and behavior of the Tidy Options panel.

  • Highlight matching braces when typing.

  • Adjust the editor colors and use color themes.

  • And more!

Explore

All of these features and more are exposed in Preferences, the menus, and the toolbar.
