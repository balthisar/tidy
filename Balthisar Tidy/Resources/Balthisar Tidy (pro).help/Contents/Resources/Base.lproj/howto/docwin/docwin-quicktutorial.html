Show the Quick Tutorial

The Quick Tutorial is a brief walkthrough of a Balthisar Tidy document window that highlights its main features. Follow the steps below to activate the Quick Tutorial.

  1. The Quick Tutorial starts automatically the first time you use Balthisar Tidy.

  2. You can also activate the Quick Tutorial by selecting Show the Quick Tutorial… in the Help menu.

  3. You can start the Quick Tutorial using the Tutorial button on the document window’s toolbar.
