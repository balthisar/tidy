Support and Bug Reports

balthisar.com wants you to be a happy user and so if you have questions, bug reports, feature suggestions, and so on, then we want to hear from you!

Although you’re always welcome to email us at balthisar@balthisar.com or communicate with us at Twitter, may we suggest you also consider the following options?

  • Ensure that you are using the most up-to-date version of Balthisar Tidy. Check the App Store to ensure that there are no pending updates awaiting your approval. The App Store can be setup to download all updates automatically.

  • Check Balthisar Tidy’s FAQs.

  • Have a look at Balthisar Tidy’s support page which always has up-to-date support information.

  • Visit our bug tracker and feature request issues list at github. This system will make it easy for you to share your problems with other users and provide up-to-the-date information about the status of your requests.
