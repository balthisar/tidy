Use the Window Splitter Controls

In addition to the ability to toggle the Tidy Options and toggle the Tidy Messages panels, You can change the relative size of every document panel by using the splitters.

  1. The splitters are impossible to see, but your mouse cursor will change shape when it passes over one of the splitters.

  2. At this point you can press and drag with the mouse to resize the adjacent areas.
