Help Us Help You!

Balthisar Tidy is used around the world, so why not help us make it available in your native language?

Those of us at balthisar.com speak a couple of languages in addition to English, but the quality isn’t nearly enough for our high standards for quality software.

Balthisar Tidy is completely free of charge, and Balthisar Tidy for Work’s primary purpose is to earn money for professional translation, but with a niche product like Balthisar Tidy you can imagine that the volumes won’t support more than a single language, or perhaps two. So what can you do, if you’re so inclined?

Help out!

The source code to Balthisar Tidy is on github. Have a look at the repository, choose a language, and help make Balthisar Tidy yours.

Buy Balthisar Tidy for Work

As mentioned earlier, Balthisar Tidy for Work is primarily intended as an avenue for pursuing funding for language localization. While we‘re happy to swallow the costs of distributing Balthisar Tidy free of charge, language translation is just too expensive for that.

Priority for translations goes by Balthisar Tidy for Work’s sales volumes in each language reason, so convince your friends to buy, too.

And remember, Balthisar Tidy for Work dangles a couple of advanced features to further encourage the investment.

Sponsor a language personally or commercially

If you or a company that you have a relationship with would like to sponsor one or more translations of Balthisar Tidy, please get in contact with us. Aside from doing a good deed, you and/or your organization will have prominent placement on this page for all posterity.
