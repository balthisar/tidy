Splitters

Splitters provide additional options for making Balthisar Tidy’s document window work according to your tastes. We’ve taken steps to mentioning them in this reference work because they’re not an obvious feature, due to the splitters being impossible to see. Your mouse cursor will change shape when it passes over one of the splitters.

When the cursor changes to this shape, you can resize the panels to the left and right of the splitter.
When the cursor appears as such, you can resize the panels above and below the splitter.
