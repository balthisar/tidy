Balthisar Tidy displays a lot of information in a single document window, and not all of this information is important to you all the time. As such the document window is resizable and reconfigurable. Even better, this configuration is remembered for you each time you open a new document, even after quitting and restarting Balthisar Tidy.

There are a total of four splitter bars between each of the panels in the document window. They may be difficult to see, but you will notice that your mouse pointer changes shape as it hovers or passes over the borders. Simply grab and drag the splitters until the view is how you want it to be.
